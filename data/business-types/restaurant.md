## Description
Premium mobile application for restaurants with features matching professional apps like McDonald's, UberEats, Glovo, Domino's. Includes digital menu, direct orders (zero commissions), table reservations, intelligent delivery, loyalty program, and AI-powered recommendations.

## Long Description
A comprehensive restaurant management app that enables restaurants to take orders directly from customers, manage reservations, handle deliveries, and build customer loyalty - all without paying third-party commissions. Features include AI-powered recommendations, dynamic delivery pricing, subscription plans, and complete admin panel for restaurant management.
