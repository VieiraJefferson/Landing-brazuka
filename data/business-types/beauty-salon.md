## Description
Complete mobile application for beauty salons and aesthetics centers, including intelligent scheduling, multi-staff management, service catalog, loyalty program, integrated product store, and AI-powered features for advanced aesthetics services.

## Long Description
A comprehensive salon management app designed for beauty salons and aesthetics centers. Includes services for hairdressing, manicure/pedicure, makeup, hair removal, advanced aesthetics, micropigmentation, eyelash extensions, facial treatments, tanning, and massage. Features intelligent scheduling, multi-staff coordination, client history tracking, loyalty programs, and subscription plans.
